1, 2, 3 to the Zoo by Eric Carle

Meet the colorful zoo animals on board the zoo choo-choo train. With their help, counting is as easy as 1-2-3. In this activity, explore counting using the zoo animals and cardboard train.

Theme: Math / Matemáticas

10 Little Rubber Ducks by Eric Carle

Ten rubber ducks are loaded onto a cargo ship, and along comes a storm that sends them overboard. Follow their adventures as each duck drifts towards a different part of the world. After you read the book, practice counting, with the included rubber ducks as you send them on an adventure of your choosing.

Theme: Math / Matemáticas

A Bad Case of Stripes by David Shannon

How will Camilla Cream change her stripes or better yet, get rid of them all together? She learns an important lesson about being herself and so will you as you complete a personal portrait with the art supplies in this kit.

Theme: Sense of Self / Autoconcepto
